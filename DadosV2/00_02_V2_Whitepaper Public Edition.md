**5. How the Protocol Works**  
  
DGTEnergy follows a deterministic and rule based workflow that ensures transparency and structural discipline.  
  
**5.1 Acquisition**  
  
Participants acquire DGT3 through the Whitelist or public rounds. The sale contract automatically routes ninety six percent to the Treasury and four percent to the Management Fee Safe.  
  
**5.2 Activation**  
  
Participants may stake their DGT3 at any time. Staking is optional and creates no lock period or financial obligation.  
  
**5.3 Deployment and Verification**  
  
The Treasury deploys capital to operational partners under predefined rules. Partners generate receivables, settle them through regulated market processes and return verified operational results.  
  
**5.4 Transparency**  
  
The protocol publishes verified operational data in real time. Staked participants gain complete access to cycle level visibility.  
  
**5.5 Exit Path**  
  
Participants may sell or transfer tokens through the DGTEnergy P2P Desk under KYC requirements. All transfers occur via escrow settlement. There is no redemption mechanism and no financial obligation from the protocol.
